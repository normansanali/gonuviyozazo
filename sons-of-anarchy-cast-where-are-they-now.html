Sons of Anarchy Cast: Where Are They Now?

August 01, 2024
blog

Sons of Anarchy hooked fans in for seven seasons from 2008 to 2014 and helped push star Charlie Hunnam to superstardom.

The FX series followed single father Jax Teller (Hunnam) as he tried to get his outlaw biker gang, Sons of Anarchy Motorcycle Club Redwood Original (SAMCRO), back on the right side of the law while keeping a few illegal side hustles here and there.

Throughout the series, Jax learns about his late father, John Teller (Nicholas Guest) and what his original vision was for the Charming, California, biker boys.

The show, which was created by Kurt Sutter, also starred Katey Sagal as Jax’s mom, Gemma, Maggie Siff as his love Tara, Ron Perlman as former SAMCRO leader Clay, Ryan Hurst as Jax’s BFF Opie and Theo Rossi as Juice.
