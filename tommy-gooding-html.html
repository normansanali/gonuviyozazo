Tommy Gooding - Age, Family, Bio

July 25, 2024
blog

Musician and director known as Cuba Gooding Jr.'s brother and Cuba Gooding Sr.'s son. He has played as a bassist and worked as a musical director.

Before Fame

He studied music at Five Towns College in Dix Hills, New York.

Trivia

He directed his father's band. He worked with bands including Circular Time, GQ, and JJ Sansavarino.

Family Life

In 2019, he married Seydurah Avecmoi. His siblings are Cuba, April Gooding and Omar Gooding. His mother is named Shirley Gooding.

Associated With

His brother Cuba won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in the 1996 film Jerry Maguire opposite Tom Cruise, Regina King and Renée Zellweger.
