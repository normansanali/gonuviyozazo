Spring Baking Championship Wiki

The Spring Baking Championship is an American cooking competition that airs on the Food Network, with a grand prize of $50,000 for the winner. Chef Bobby Deen originally hosted the show, and the judges were Nancy Fuller, Duff Goldman and Lorraine Pascale. The first season premiered on April 25, 2015, followed by a second season on April 10, 2016. Jesse Palmer took over as host for the third season and Ali Khan for the fourth season. Clinton Kelly hosted the fifth season and Khan returned as host for the seventh season with Kardea Brown replacing Pascale as judge. The eighth season was hosted by Molly Yeh, and the ninth season has Jesse Palmer returning as host from March 6, 2023.
