Are Dove Cameron And Veronica St Clair Dating?

blog

Dove Cameron, an American actress, is one of the most-followed people on Instagram, with over 46.5 million followers.

The 26-year-old rose to prominence on the Disney Channel as the star of the popular show Liv and Maddie, for which she received a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Performer in Children’s Programming.

She has since amassed a massive fan base as a result of her roles in Descendents, Dumplin’, Marvel’s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., and a successful music career.

This week, she shared a cozy photo with actress Veronica St Clair, leaving fans perplexed about their relationship status. Are they in a relationship? Let us find out…

Dove Posts Photo With Veronica

On Wednesday (April 6th), the former Disney Channel star took to Instagram to share a photo with Veronica St Clair. She is best known for her role as Riley in NBC’s La Brea, but she has also appeared in 13 Reasons Why and Unbelievable. The couple appears to be at an airport or train station in the photo, holding hands and touching their heads together.

“My girl, my world,” Dove wrote in the caption. From 8,000 miles away, I wish you a happy birthday. We’ll be together forever.”

Are They Dating?

The adorable birthday post has sparked speculation that the couple is dating because they appear to be very close, but this is not the case.

Dove and Veronica are simply best friends. In fact, best friends are soulmates or even sisters. We don’t know how they met, but they presumably met in show business and have since formed a strong friendship. A quick glance at their Instagram profiles reveals that they’ve known each other for years.

The actress shared a few photos from her birthday night out in April 2021, writing, “It’s my best friend’s birthday week and she’s 1/2 the world away on her big grand adventure.”

“Here are some photos of me crying on her for 30 minutes at a party full of people a few weeks ago, telling her I would die for her.” “Veronica, you are my most treasured,” she went on.

“You are the pinnacle of what a human being can aspire to be.” You are my entire world. I can’t believe you’re here. I am extremely fortunate, as is the entire world. Baby, have a wonderful birthday. “I’ll see you soon.”

Veronica has also shared photos of herself with Dove on Instagram, and it appears that their bond is unbreakable.

“Happy Birthday, my Chlo!!!” she wrote in a birthday post from 2020. To me, you are moonlight swims, Parisian sunrises, tears of joy, giant spoonfuls of tapioca pudding, and laughing very hard, very often, and sometimes at inopportune times (hehehe). “I adore you.”
