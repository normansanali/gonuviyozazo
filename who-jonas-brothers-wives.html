Who are the Jonas Brothers' wives?

JONAS Brothers fans were left stunned after reports claimed Joe Jonas had filed for divorce from wife Sophie Turner.

But pop music enthusiasts that tuned into Kevin, Nick, and Joe Jonas are now curious to know more about the remaining JoBros' marriages.

Who are the Jonas Brothers' wives?

Three brothers - Kevin, Nick and Joe Jonas - formed pop band The Jonas Brothers in 2005.

The threesome went on to have huge success with hits such as Burnin' Up, Sucker, SOS, and Lovebug.

As well as their music the handsome trio pulled in numerous fans thanks to their good looks and TV roles, with lots of interest surrounding their love lives.

Here's what we know about their spouses...

Danielle Deleasa

At 22 years old, Kevin Jonas was the first of the brothers to officially get married.

On December 19, 2009, Kevin and hairdresser Danielle Deleasa tied the knot after two years of dating.

The two held their wedding ceremony at Oheka Castle in Huntington, New York.

Regarding how he and Danielle met, the singer told Glamour: "You know how you're supposed to meet somebody and call like two or three days later?

"Well, I cyberstalked her after we met and cyberstalked to see what flight she was going to be on coming home."

The former Disney actor added: "Then, I called her the minute she landed.

"That might have been a little overboard, but I just went for it."

Eldest brother Kevin was also the first of the Jonas Brothers to have children.

In February 2014, he and Danielle welcomed their first child together, a daughter named Alena.

Two years later in October 2016, the pair welcomed another daughter named Valentina.

Danielle launched her own jewelry line in December 2018 after founding the Danielle Jonas Co.

In 2022 the couple published their first children's picture book together, There's A Rock Concert In My Bedroom.

In July 2023, they released their second book - There's A Beach In My Bedroom - which became an Amazon No1 seller.

Priyanka Chopra

Nick and Joe waited nearly a decade after their older brother Kevin to make their commitment in marriage.

In December 2018, younger JoBro Nick married model, actress, and fellow singer Priyanka Chopra.

Their wedding ceremony took place in Priyanka's home country, India.

Nick and Priyanka's marriage came on the heels of dating since May of that year.

In January 2022, the wedded pair announced the arrival of their first child together, a baby girl named Malti Marie, via surrogate.

Prior to the couple's union, Nick previously dated Miley Cyrus and Selena Gomez, as well as fashion and social media influencer Olivia Culpo.

Priyanka was crowned Miss World in November 2000, and went on to star in the Indian film industry.

SHe achieved Bollywood success in films Andaaz,  Mujhse Shaadi Karogi,  Krrish, and Don.

In 2013, she voiced Ishani in Planes, and appeared as Alex Parrish in Quantico from 2015 until 2018.

As well as starring in her husband's music videos, Priyanka has starred in The Matrix Revolutions and Love Again.

She joined the cast of TV series Citadel as Nadia Sinh in 2023.

Sophie Turner

In the wake of his romances with Demi Lovato, Taylor Swift, and Gigi Hadid, middle brother Joe Jonas went on to date Sophie Turner.

Joe and Game Of Thrones actress Sophie struck up a relationship in 2016.

They became engaged in October 2017 and said "I do" on May 1, 2019, with their nuptials taking place in Las Vegas, Nevada.

On July 22, 2020, the DNCE frontman and X-Men star announced the arrival of their first child, a baby girl named Willa.

On March 1, 2022, it was revealed that the star couple was expecting another child.

While speaking to InTouch Weekly, a source told the publication: "Sophie is a private person and won’t make an official announcement about the pregnancy until she’s ready.

"She is almost halfway through. They are absolutely ecstatic and can’t wait for Willa to have a sibling."

The insider disclosed: "Picking a name, which they aren’t sharing with anyone until the baby arrives, is what they’re really excited about."

On July 14, 2022, it was announced that Joe and Sophie had welcomed their second daughter together.

But just a year later, the marriage seemed to have gone south and Joe filed for divorce from Sophie in September 2023 in Miami.

According to The Blast, the prenuptial agreement states that Joe will “keep all of his royalties from the music” with his siblings, while Sophie will keep the money she made from Game Of Thrones as well as “any residuals from her acting career”.

A source shed some light on Joe's reasoning for his decision, telling the outlet: "She likes to party, he likes to stay at home. They have very different lifestyles."

According to Page Six, Joe tried to "salvage" his four-year marriage before ending the relationship.

It said: “Divorce was a last resort for Joe. He never wanted to break up his family, but he had to take what he felt was the best course of action for his girls."
