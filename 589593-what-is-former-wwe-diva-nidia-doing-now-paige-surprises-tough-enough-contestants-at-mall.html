Nidia Nowadays

WWE’s latest “Where Are They Now” feature catches up with the co-winner of Tough Enough season 1, Nidia Guenard. Nidia debuted on the main roster in 2002 and released in November of 2004.

Currently, she leads a Girl Scouts troop in her free time and is married with two kids. She talks about being a stay-at-home mom and gave advice to the current Tough Enough hopefuls:

“For me, I didn’t think I was going to be a mom, but after I decided that was the route we were going to take, it was very important for me to stay home. My parents didn’t stay home, but I was lucky that my grandparents took care of me. It was nice for me to have such a loving environment and someone who was always looking out for my best interest. That’s what I wanted to do for my kids.”

“Make sure that you love it if you want to do it. It’s definitely a profession that you have to love if you want to succeed. It’s not easy.”
