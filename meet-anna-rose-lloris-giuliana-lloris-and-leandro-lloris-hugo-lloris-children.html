Hugo Hadrien Dominique Lloris is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur.

However, this article will not focus on his undoubtedly successful career. Today we will take a look at a separate part of his life, his family with his beautiful wife Marine Lloris.

Meet Anna Rose Lloris, Giuliana Lloris and Leandro Lloris, Hugo Lloris children

Hugo Lloris is a proud family man. The talented football player has welcomed three wonderful kids with his wife, Marine Lloris.

The happy couple met in 2002, while he was studying at the Lycée Thierry Maulnier. A decade later, they tied the knot at the Église Saint-François-de-Paule in Nice.

However, the couple welcomed their first child, a daughter named Anna-Rose, on September 23, 2010, two years earlier.

In the following years, the family quickly grew their number from 3 to 5. Their second daughter, Giuliana was born in 2014, and their son Léandro was born on September 20, 2019.
