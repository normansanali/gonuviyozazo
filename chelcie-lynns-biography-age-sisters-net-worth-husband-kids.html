Chelcie Lynn’s Biography

Her real name is Chelcie Lynn, as there has always been disagreement on her real name; she was born on the 9th of August 1987 in Oklahoma in the US. She would, however, relocate to San Diego, CA, US.

Her grandmother raised her, this is because her parents were alcoholics and meth addicts. The reports revealed that her mother was arrested for drug possession and died in prison from cervical cancer, her father, on the other hand, went to rehab and later remarried.

Chelcie has two sisters named Beth and Maggie, both of whom appear in many of her videos.

Education

The reports revealed she finished from a local high school but didn’t proceed to college as there was no financial backup. She then moved to San Diego, where she worked in a biomedical engineering company as a tester but left after two weeks to pursue a career in comedy.

Career

Chelcie started uploading comic videos on now-defunct social media platform Vine in 2013. The comedian in the videos played the character Trailer Trash Tammy, a comedic representation of ‘redneck’ women from the American south. She would, however, move to YouTube in 2015.

With her numerous videos, she has built huge followership on various social media platforms with over one million followers, and some of her videos amass over 130 million views.

She has also acted in a number of Hollywood movies which include, Dog Eat Dog, Tangerine, Coach Von Pidgeon, among others.

Relationship

She is married to Greg Melton, who also features in her comic videos. The two got married on July 14, 2009. They are secretive about their personal life; thereofore, it is unknown if they have a child of their own.
