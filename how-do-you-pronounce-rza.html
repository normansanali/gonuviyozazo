How do you pronounce RZA?

September 20, 2024
blog

With the due date of Rihanna’s second child with rapper A$AP Rocky fast approaching, the pair finally revealed what they named their firstborn, whom they welcomed into the world about a year ago.

The name that they ended up going with, as we’re sure you’re aware since you’re here on this article, is RZA – after one of the members of the timelessly popular hip-hop group, the Wu-Tang Clan. While it may seem like a very Hollywood thing to do, it actually somewhat carries on a family tradition of Rocky’s. 

Nevertheless, a name that is lacking vowels may prove a tricky hurdle for babysitters, school teachers, heck – even the DMV in a decade and a half, plus change. So, this begs the question – how exactly do you pronounce the superstar couple’s child’s name, if not you’re not just straight-up spelling out ‘’Argh-zee-aye?” Let’s dig in. 

RZA pronunciation, explained

Given Rihanna and Rocky’s firstborn is confirmed to be named after the legendary Wu-Tang Clan member of the same name, it’s very safe to presume that RZA is going to be pronounced ‘Rizza.’ We can cite countless video interviews from RZA’s decades-spanning career as a reference, but in reality we’d be here all day – far and wide, it’s pretty common knowledge that RZA = Rizza.
