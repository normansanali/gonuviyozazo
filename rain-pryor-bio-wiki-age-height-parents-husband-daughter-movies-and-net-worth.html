Acting and Comedy

Pryor is a multi-talented American artist who has made a name for herself in the entertainment industry as an actress, comedian, writer, and producer. She has appeared in various films, TV shows, and voice roles, such as How the Grinch Stole Christmas, Shameless Women, and Tigra.

Pryor’s career as an actress began in 1988 when she starred in the ABC series Head of the Class in the role of Theola June ‘T.J.’ Jones. Her role was created from a series of characters she performed at her audition for the producers.
