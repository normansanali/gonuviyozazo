Does Patrick Mahomes own a team in the MLS?

August 03, 2024
blog

Patrick Mahomes, the superstar quarterback of the Kansas City Chiefs and a prominent figure in the sports world, has made significant moves into sports ownership. 

Patrick Mahomes' Ownership in Sporting Kansas City

In July 2021, it was announced that Patrick Mahomes had become a member of the Sporting Club ownership group, which owns Sporting Kansas City, a prominent MLS team.

Mahomes expressed his gratitude for the opportunity and his excitement to support the growth of soccer in Kansas City.

Cliff Illig, the principal owner of Sporting KC, welcomed Mahomes into the ownership group, emphasizing Mahomes' commitment to giving back to the community and elevating the sport of soccer.

Mahomes' Impact

Mahomes, known for his excellence on the football field, has also shown dedication to cultivating a winning culture in sports.

His involvement in the ownership group is expected to contribute to Sporting Kansas City's efforts to contend for championships at Children’s Mercy Park in the future.

Patrick Mahomes' Ownership in NWSL's KC Current

In addition to his ownership in Sporting Kansas City, Patrick Mahomes and his wife, Brittany Mahomes, have extended their ownership endeavors to the NWSL by becoming part owners of the KC Current soccer team.

Their ownership stake in the KC Current represents a significant expansion of their sports ownership portfolio, reflecting their commitment to supporting and uplifting professional sports teams in Kansas City.

Family Involvement

Brittany Mahomes, a former professional soccer player, is part of the ownership group alongside asset managers Angie and Chris Long. This move has been described as a "family affair," with Patrick being a huge supporter of the Current behind the scenes.

Achievements and Future Plans

The KC Current has made notable strides in the NWSL, reaching the championship in only their second season despite finishing as the fifth seed in the regular season.

Also, the team has broken ground on the first stadium in the U.S. solely dedicated to a women’s professional soccer team, with an expected completion before the 2024 season.

Mahomes' Commitment to Sports Ownership

Patrick Mahomes' involvement in sports ownership extends beyond his role as a star athlete.

His commitment to the growth and success of professional sports teams is evident in his ownership stake in Sporting Kansas City and the KC Current.

Adding to that, his establishment of the 15 and the Mahomies Foundation in 2019 reflects his dedication to improving the lives of children, showcasing a holistic approach to making a positive impact beyond the field.

 

As a KC sports icon, Patrick Mahomes' ownership in MLS and NWSL teams signifies his multifaceted involvement in the sports industry.

His contributions to Sporting Kansas City and the KC Current highlight his commitment to elevating soccer and professional sports in Kansas City, while also demonstrating his dedication to giving back to the community.

As we witness the evolution of Patrick Mahomes' sports ownership endeavors, it is evident that his involvement in MLS and NWSL teams is a testament to his vision for the growth and success of sports in Kansas City and beyond.
