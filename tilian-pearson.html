Tilian Pearson- Biography

Tilian Pearson was born in Clearwater, FL on July 12, 1987. Known otherwise by the mononym Tilian, he first rose to fame as a member of the rock band Tides of Man. He has since become more widely known as the clean vocalist of the post-hardcore band Dance Gavin Dance. In 2016, he and Jonny Craig released the collaborative single “Back to Life.”

That same year, he joined the artist on his Spring U.S. Tour. His professional music career began in December 2007 when he and the rest of the founding members of Tides of Man got together at a show in Tampa and began writing songs together.
