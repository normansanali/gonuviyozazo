Batista Discusses His Role as Mr. Hinx in Spectre, Big Push Planned for ROH Stars in New Japan

September 13, 2024

Batista Talks 007: Spectre

EmpireOnline.com has a few quotes from former WWE Superstar Dave Bautista and 29-year-old French actress Lea Seydoux about their roles in the upcoming James Bond movie, Spectre. 

“When I came over to meet Sam [Mendes], I only asked two questions. I asked him if Mr. Hinx was a badass. He said, ‘Yes, he’s a badass.’ I said, ‘Well, is Mr. Hinx intelligent?’ He said, ‘Very.’ That’s what I like about Mr. Hinx. He’s very well-dressed and very well-mannered. I’m not just here to fight people. He has a sense of humour. He definitely knows what a metaphor is.”

ROH Stars Pushed in New Japan?

Dave Meltzer is reporting that Ring of Honor stars Michael Bennett and Matt Taven are expected to receive a big push in New Japan Pro Wrestling.

Bennett and Taven have been two of the few stars benefiting in a big way from the partnership from the two companies, and made a trip to Japan between November and December wrestling in the G1 Tag League, finishing with a respectable 8 points. There’s also been talk of Bennett receiving a strong singles push with the promotion.
