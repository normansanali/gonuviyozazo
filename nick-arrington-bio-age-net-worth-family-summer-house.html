Nick Arrington has style. He’s got the standard. And now, he’s in the spotlight, thanks to his time on the Summer House spinoff, Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard.

Get to know him better as this Nick Arrington Bio proceeds.

Nick Arrington On Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard

Bravo’s Summer House is known for its relationship drama, friendship scuffles, and love triangles. So, add all exclusive Black entrepreneurs to the list, you’ve got Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard. “If you don’t know, Black excellence is Martha’s Vineyard, and Martha’s Vineyard is Black excellence,” one of the cast members says in the trailer.

And she’s 100 percent true! Martha’s Vineyard actually has a long history of hosting Black vacationers, and it was “one of the first beach destinations where African Americans could vacation and purchase property.”

Our star, Nick Arrington is one of those 11 Black entrepreneurs sharing a space during the hottest months of the year.

“So it begins…#SummerHouseMV premieres TONIGHT at 9 pm EST / 8 PM CST on @BravoTV & @peacock I hope you enjoy the show this Summer! A little look 👀 at what’s to come. I’ll be dressed for every scene!” he wrote on FB on the premiere day.

Also, he thanked all the well-wishers, “PS Thank you to everyone that came out to our premier party this week in NYC 🗽! I look forward to watching tonight with my Mother and family at home in Virginia! To my cast and Bravo Family…cheers to US!”

Nick Arrington Job

Nick is a seasoned marketing professional with a focus on brand/sponsorship activation and event delivery that stretches across multiple industries and countries.

In 2023, he was working as a brand manager at Deloitte for almost 6 years. His team managed the National Sponsorship Portfolio of the firm, and its relationships with the United States Golf Association (USGA), Tennis Association (USTA), Soccer Federation (USSF), Olympic & Paralympic Committee (USOPC), and James Beard Foundation (JBF).

Before Deloitte, Nick worked as a Marketing Consultant at J.P. Morgan for seven months. There, he oversaw the delivery of annual/ad-box C-Level client entertainment/thought leadership events across America’s footprint.

Also, he took a similar job before at Barclays for almost 6 years, managing event marketing as it relates to Wimbledon, Rugby World Cup, The Barclays ATP Tennis Tournament, Barclays Premier League, and opening The Barclays Center.

But Nick’s not just a corporate man, he’s also dabbled in advertisement and has modeled for J. Crew, Brooks Brothers, Duchamp London, and other household name brands.

When Nick isn’t working he loves to run marathons. So far, he has run the New York, London, and Berlin marathons.

As for his education, he earned his double Bachelor of Science degrees in Sales & Marketing and Business Administration from Tuskegee University in 2008.

At Tuskegee, he was a scholarship athlete and co-captain of Tuskegee’s baseball team. Also, he served as 1st Gentlemen to Mr. Tuskegee and pledged Kappa Alpha Psi, Fraternity Inc.

Nick Arrington Age

Nick Arrington was 37 years of age when he appeared on Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard in 2023.

However, he was a year younger when he filmed the show.

Nick Arrington Family

Nick Arrington comes from a family of five.

Amongst them, it’s clear that Nick loved his mother Linda Arrington the most as she was often featured on his socials. He shared that it was his mother who taught him to be “caring, polite, grateful and to take pride in the way I present myself.”

When Nick was a child, she used to tell him, “If you leave my house looking any kind of way, people are going to treat you any kind of way.” Those words have stuck with him to this day.

On Linda’s birthday on March 20, 2014, Nick took to his FB to write, “You taught me everything, And everything you’ve given me I always keep it inside. Happy Birthday to Mother Arrington! She is aging like fine wine! Those of you who know me personally, know this is the woman that taught me everything!!! She is my biggest fan, mentor, shopping buddy, Mother, Father, and even my spades partner!”

Guess, Nick’s father was out of the picture.

As for his siblings, Nick has a brother named Norm Delaney and a sister named Lindsi Arrington.

Lindsi is a Florida International University graduate who went on to be the Publications Manager at her alma mater. Now, she’s a Videographer at REDCON1.

Is Nick Arrington Dating Anyone?

Since Nick Arrington was entering Summer House, he was definitely single, and judging from the trailer he might have mingled with someone from the house.

Bravo explained him to be a self-proclaimed romantic who loves hard and has high standards when it comes to dating. “He insists that his future wife must be in shape and have a skincare routine that matches his own,” they added.
