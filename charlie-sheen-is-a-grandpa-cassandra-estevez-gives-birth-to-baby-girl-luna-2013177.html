Charlie Sheen Is a Grandpa! Cassandra Estevez Gives Birth to Baby Girl

September 12, 2024
blog

Charlie Sheen is officially a grandpa! The Anger Management actor's oldest daughter, Cassandra Estevez, welcomed her first child with husband Casey Huffman on Wednesday, July 17, he told TMZ.

Estevez, 28, who is Sheen's daughter with high school sweetheart Paula Speert, gave birth to a baby girl named Luna. "It's impossibly a most wondrous day," Sheen, 47, told TMZ of becoming a grandfather. "My bucket list is a thimble!"
