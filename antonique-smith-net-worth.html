Antonique Smith is an American actress. She’s also a singer. She had her career beginnings in the early 2000s, debuting with a recurring role in 100 Centre Street. She next appeared in an episode of Law & Order. Later in 2007, she had her first movie role in Across the Universe. Two years later, she portrayed Faith Evans in the biographical drama film Notorious. In the 2010s, Smith had performances in the feature films Yelling to the Sky, Abduction, Stock Option, and Deuces. She went on to play recurring characters Kiana Ward in Shots Fired and Detective Nandi Tyler in Marvel’s Luke Cage.
