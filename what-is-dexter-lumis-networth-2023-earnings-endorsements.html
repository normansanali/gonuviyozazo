Samuel Robert Shaw (born January 17, 1984, Jacksonville, Florida, United States) is an American professional wrestler who is currently signed to WWE and performs under the ring name Dexter Lumis. He is also well-known for his tenure with TNA, where he appeared from 2010 to 2015 under the ring names Sam Shaw and Samuel Shaw.

Lumis began his professional wrestling career in 2007 on the independent circuit. He joined Total Nonstop Action Wrestling in 2010, winning the TNA Gut Check challenge in 2012. He subsequently spent time in Ohio Valley Wrestling before coming back in 2013. He left TNA in 2015 and spent the subsequent five years competing in independent wrestling until joining WWE in 2019. After being released in April 2022, he went back to the indie scene and joined WWE again in August of the same year.

He graduated in 2006 from Savannah College of Art & Design and Allen D. Nease High School in St. Augustine, Florida. Lumis traveled to Orlando to work as an illustrator for Universal Studios, then returned to Jacksonville to start drawing caricatures for the Riverside Arts Market. He also ran a side business sketching people’s dogs on Christmas ornaments.

Dexter Lumis in WWE

Shaw secured a deal with WWE and would go to the WWE Performance Center on February 11, 2019. On March 16, he competed in an NXT house show and lost to Fabian Aichner. Lumis was defeated by Bronson Reed in the opening round of the competition.

He helped Velveteen Dream defeat The Undisputed Era in a tag team match after Dream’s partner, Keith Lee, was eliminated by Damian Priest. In a subsequent attempt to help Dream during his NXT Championship bout against Adam Cole on the May 6 episode of NXT, Lumis mistakenly knocked out the referee, costing Dream the match and the title.
