Rob Lowe Biography

Robert Hepler Lowe is an actor, producer, and director in the United States. He won the Primetime Emmy Award nominations and two Golden Globe Awards. He has also appeared on ABC drama Brothers & Sisters (2006-2010), NBC sitcom Parks & Recreation (2010-2014, 2015), Code Black medical drama (2016-2018), and the FOX play 9-1-1: Lone Star (2020–present). In 2018, he made his debut as a director in the TV film The Bad Seed, a reworking of the 1956 film of the same name.
