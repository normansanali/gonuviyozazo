It looks like Kaia Gerber and Pete Davidson are most definitely a thing.

If there was any doubt before, that's probably been extinguished now that the pair has been photographed on vacation together in Miami Beach, Florida.

While Gerber looked stunning in a tiny black bikini, Davidson wore a pair of bathing shorts. From getting up close and personal on their sun loungers to full-on touching tongues in public to canoodling in the swimming pool, Davidson and Gerber both looked pretty smitten.

Rumors about the 18-year-old model and the 26-year-old comedian started swirling back in October when they were spotted grabbing lunch together in New York. At the time, a source told Page Six that Gerber and Davidson met when they both walked the runway for Alexander Wang in New York on May 31, 2019.

More recently, Davidson invited Gerber to the Saturday Night Live afterparty in New York, which doubled as his birthday party. A source told Us Weekly, "Pete and Kaia, they showed up together, but did not hold hands."

Now that they've been spotted engaging in a pretty serious make-out session, it seems as though something is most definitely going on.
