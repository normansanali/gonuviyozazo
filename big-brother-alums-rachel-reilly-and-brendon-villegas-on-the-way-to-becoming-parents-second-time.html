Another Celebrity Mom on the Way! Big Brother Alums Rachel Reilly and Brendon Villegas Expecting Sec

From Big Brother to getting married and now being parents for the second time; Rachel Reilly and Brendon Villegas are showering love for each other for some time now. 

The couple is already parents to one daughter, Adora Borealis who is now four-years-old. In the midst of this COVID-19 pandemic, Rachel is another celebrity after Ashlee Simpson and Katherine Schwarzenegger expecting a child.

In an exclusive report published by People Magazine, the duo was trying for a second child for some time now. While sharing her exciting news, Rachel also talked about the Ava Women app and how she tracked her ovulation cycle with it and got pregnant.

Brendon showed his concern over the unborn baby and his pregnant wife due to Coronavirus Pandemic. He said,

"In the midst of a pandemic, I am most concerned with Rachel and the baby both maintaining their health throughout the pregnancy. Honestly, that is all I care for at the moment, their safety".

Apart from the pair, another person who got really excited to hear such a piece of good news is their daughter, Adora. She screamed on becoming a big sister and the pair says, she's been asking for a baby sometime now.

In April 2016, Rachel gave birth to Adora and while she was pregnant with her, Reilly felt sick and emotional. Something similar is happening in this pregnancy as well for Rachel as she explained to People Magazine. But Adora is being a very good daughter.

While Rachel feels morning sickness, her daughter comes to the bathroom and rubs her back. Rachel said,

"She brings me water to drink all day and she even goes and grabs water bottles and tells me I have to drink them for the baby. She has made this even more exciting".

After appearing in the season 12 of Big Brother in 2010, the couple made the instant connection. And nearly after two years in September 2012, the pair walked down the wedding aisle. They were joined by their loved ones at the AT&T Center in downtown Los Angeles.

For the big day, Rachel arrived via a helicopter. At the ceremony, in the amazing backdrop of the sunset, she exchanged her vows with Brendon and vice-versa.
