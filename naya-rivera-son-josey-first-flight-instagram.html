Naya Rivera Shares Hilarious Photo from Her Son's First Flight

July 23, 2024
blog

We're sure all new moms are worried about their child's first time on an airplane, and that was certainly the case for Naya Rivera.

The former Glee star took to Instagram today to share a hilarious photo in celebration of Flashback Friday from her son Josey's first flight, and she looks more afraid than he does in the cute snap. The image shows the 29-year-old actress making an extremely concerned face as she takes a selfie while her little guy—who turns one years old next month—peacefully rests on her lap while wearing a heart print hoodie. And Rivera event admitted that she was indeed more wound up than her tiny cutie, captioning the picture: "#fbf to Joseys first flight. Clearly I was more nervous."

The tyke is no stranger to Instagram. Since sharing the first photos of Josey on the social media platform back in November, the proud mom has posted plenty of sweet 'grams of her baby boy.
