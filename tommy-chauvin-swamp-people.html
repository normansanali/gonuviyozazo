Early Life:

Tommy Chauvin was born on September 5, 1978, in Louisiana, United States. Tommy Chauvin does all the talking with his hunting. The man hasn’t revealed many details about his early life. Professional Career:

Nevertheless to Tommy, the Louisianna swamp is home and time and often he heads back to Swamp in search of a hunt.

Swamp People:

Swamp People is a History Channel reality show which follows the lives of several families who make their living catching alligators in the swamps of Louisiana, and over the years the show has introduced faces like Troy Landry, Terral Evans, Willie Edwards, Daniel Edgar, Ashley Jones, Big Tee, Dusty Crum, Liz Cavalier, Ronnie Adams.

In the 3rd season of the series, Tommy was once again compelled to strike out solo. LaFont could no longer accompany him for the hunting season due to back problems.
