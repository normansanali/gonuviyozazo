Cindy McCain Favors Joe Biden In Upcoming Presidential Election

September 23, 2024
blog

Cindy McCain, an American businesswoman, and philanthropist is with Joe Biden in the upcoming Presidential election in November 2020. McCain, a widow of a longtime Republican Senator, John McCain, announced through a Twitter that she endorses Democratic presidential nominee Joe Biden.

The endorsement came after the appearance of McCain in a video about Joe Biden's relationship with her late husband, John McCain, at the Democratic National Convention back in August 2020.

In a series of tweets, she mentioned:

'My husband John lived by a code: country first. We are Republicans, yes, but Americans foremost. There's only one candidate in this race who stands up for our values as a nation, and that is @JoeBiden.'

Similarly, she added:

"Joe and I don't always agree on the issues, and I know he and John certainly had some passionate arguments, but he is a good and honest man. He will lead us with dignity."

'He will be a commander in chief that the finest fighting force in the history of the world can depend on because he knows what it is like to send a child off to fight,' she added in the following tweet.

Similarly, Biden thanked her with a tweet later. He mentioned, 'Cindy — I'm deeply honored to have your support and your friendship. This election is bigger than any one political party. It requires all of us to come together as one America to restore the soul of the nation. Together, we'll get it done.'

Cindy McCain Net Worth

Cindy McCain is a businesswoman with tight pockets. Being a millionaire is not a big deal for McCain as she has been through several business ventures throughout the years. As of now, McCain has a net worth of $400 million.

She inherited her family's business company named Hensley & Co. in 2000 and has been serving as its chairman after her father died. The company with a massive fortune employs more than 1200 employees, as of now. The company is the third-largest Anheuser-Busch distributor in the United States and stands as one of the largest private companies.

Besides that, Cindy McCain serves on the boards of HALO Trust, Operation Smile, CARE, and many others. Also, she is a co-chair on Arizona Governor Jan Brewer's Task Force on Human Trafficking.

Who Is Cindy McCain?

Cindy McCain is an American businesswoman, humanitarian, and philanthropist. She is widely known as the chair of her family business company, Hensley & Co. She is also a widow of a longtime Republican senator, John McCain.

McCain was born on 20 May 1954 in Phoenix, Arizona, in the United States. She is a graduate of the University of Southern California, from where she garnered the degree of Bachelors and Masters in Arts.
