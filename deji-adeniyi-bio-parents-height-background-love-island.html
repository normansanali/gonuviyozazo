Deji Adeniyi On Love Island

Love Island wouldn’t be Love Island if it wasn’t for the Casa Amor twist. Infact, many viewers await this moment for it brings all the spices to the mix. But this time, it was the girls who were sent to a second villa while the boys stayed in the original one. Yes, the last we saw this happen was in 2019.

So, as the ladies joined the second villa, there awaited six brand new boys including our charmer Deji Adeniyi, all ready to build up a new relationship or even destroy the existing ones.

The show teased Deji’s arrival towards the end of the July 1st episode.

According to the star, he was taking the show seriously and was 100% competitive. Also, the charmer insisted that he wore his heart on his sleeve in the villa. Or in his words, “I’m 100% competitive – I think that is what fuels me on a daily basis. I’m my own competition. I’m hoping I’ll bring an energy that makes people realize that love is real! A lot of people take the power of love for granted and I hope people are more open to their sensitive side.”

Joining Deji for the Casa Amor twist were his co-stars George Tasker (a laborer from the Cotswolds), Samuel Agbiji (a model from Manchester), Billy Brown (a roofing company director from Surrey), Josh Le Grove (a model from Essex), and Jack Keating (the son of Ronan Keating).

But it wasn’t just the boys who joined the villa to shake things up. They were also followed by six new girls to keep things “even.”

Though Deji joined Love Island in July, it premiered on June 6, 2022, and there appeared its initial cast Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu, Afia Tonkmor, Amber Beckford, Luca Bish, Andrew La Page, Liam Llewellyn, and more.

Deji Adeniyi Age

Deji Adeniyi was 25 years of age when he appeared on Love Island in 2022.

His Casa Amor co-stars Josh and Samuel were 22, and Jack, George, and Billy were 23.
