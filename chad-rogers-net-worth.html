Chad Rogers Net Worth

Chad Rogers is an American realtor with a net worth of $10 million. He graduated from the University of Denver with a degree in Business and focused on a career in real estate upon returning to California. He established himself as a formidable real estate agent and became popular with celebrities, business people, and high-profile athletes. Joining the real estate brokerage firm, Hilton & Hyland, his career took off and he set real estate records in 2010, netting over $80 million in sales. He also sold the highest priced property in Los Angeles County that year, at $23.5 million. His success led to consultation work with multiple publications, including the Wall Street Journal, The Los Angeles Times, InTouch Weekly, The Chicago Tribune, Hot Properties, and Forbes Magazine. He became nationally recognized after appearing on the Bravo reality series, “Million Dollar Listing” for two seasons before his schedule became too hectic to continue.
