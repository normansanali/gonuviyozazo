Mark Dice Gold Coin

Dice cashed in his coin, proving to YouTube viewers that authentic gold really is worth a lot of money. He has done more than just confronting Fed police to promote sound money, however. In order to spread the word about the value of gold as well as precious metals, he has tried numerous times to sell his one-ounce gold coin to random people on the street.

He has even offered the coin to people for twenty or thirty dollars, a small fraction of its real value, and several times has offered it for free. Nearly everyone has denied the offer, however, proving Dice’s point that not many people know the true value of gold.

After several months of confusing people with the gold coin, Dice finally ended the fun.
