Moneybagg Yo (born September 23, 1991) is an American Rapper, Songwriter, Social Media Personality, and Instagram Star from Memphis, Tennessee. He is currently signed to labels like Diplomat Records under Def Jam and Collective Music Group. His real full birth name is Demario DeWayne White, Jr.

Furthermore, the rapper started his career by releasing his debut mixtape “From Da Block 2 Da Booth” on April 16, 2012. Later, the same year, Demario came up with his second project “October 20th”.

Moreover, for his next mixtape “La Familia“, the rapper collaborated with Slpy. It released on February 3, 2014. White then published a revision of the first installment of Federal mixtape titled “Federal Reloaded“.

In fact, it also featured American rappers OG Boo Dirty and Young Dolph. on June 7, 2016, he aired another mixtape titled “ELO” (Everybody Lives On) featuring American rapper Yo Gotti.

In his career, the rapper earned Memphis Hip Hop Award for Mixtape of the Year award. Demario’s eleventh mixtape titled “Heartless” debuted at No. 181 position on the US Billboard 200 chart.

It came out on February 14, 2017. Later, his next project “Federal 3X” also peaked at No. 5 on the US Billboard 200 chart and No. 4 on the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) chart. Some of other Yo’s best projects are “2 Heartless”, “NLess Ent x Bread Gang”, and “Fed Baby’s”.
